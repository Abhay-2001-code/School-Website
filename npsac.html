    School Academics

    Academics



    Our teachers and facilitators at the schools are qualified, professional and committed individuals dedicated to overall development of the child's personality, skills and ability. Which is in accordance with our commitment to focus on individualistic needs rather than the generic instructional methodology. Each classroom accommodates only 35 students and the schools maintain a Student :Teacher ratio of 21:1.Teaching in schools is student-centric, activity-oriented and is an Experiential Learning. Practical Teaching Techniques with Props, Abacus Method of teaching Mathematics is followed in the school. International exchange programs that are partnered by NPS' ensure global exposure through interactions with academicians, students and professionals from other countries.The results of the school in terms of the accomplishments of the students of class X are testimony to the relentless efforts of the faculty.At NPS', the teaching patterin is divided into two meticulous and ordered arrangement: the Primary and Secondary section. The Primary section consists of 1-4th grade students. It consists a total of 15 teachers. The Secondary section consists of 5-10th students. A total of 34 highly qualified teachers handle this section.



    Primary Section



    Subjects taught to the primary section students are English, Environmental Studies, Hindi, Mathematics, Science and Social Studies. Extra-curricular activites such as singing and dancing classes are introduced at this point to unleash a sense of creativity and independence in the little minds.Interactive sessions, role-plays, project works, cooperative learning, discussions, visual and audio media are played to the students to enhance a social and practical approach of learning the subjects in the school are encouraged a lot.














    Secondary Section



    The subjects taught in Std 5 are English, Kannada (2nd language), Hindi / Sanskrit / French (3rd language), Social Studies, Science, Maths, Computers, SUPW, GK, Yoga and Physical Education.The subjects taught in Std 6 are English, Kannada (2nd language), Hindi / Sanskrit / French (3rd language), History, Geography, Science, Maths, Computers, SUPW, GK, Yoga, and Physical Education. The subjects taught in Std 7 are English, Hindi (2nd language), Marathi / Konkani / Portuguese (3rd language), History, Geography, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Maths,Computers, SUPW, Yoga and Physical Education.The subjects taught for students from 8-10th grade are English, Kannada / Hindi / French (2nd language), Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History, Geography, SUPW, Yoga, and PE. For classes 9 and 10, we offer the students a choice of Computer Applications, Commercial Applications, Physical Education, Art, Environmental Applications and Western Music as their 6th subject.The teaching methods are basically towards preparing them gradually for the Board Examination. Aids like maps, charts, CD presentations, OHP presentations, dramatization, etc are used to enhance the teaching-learning process.For those students who are weak in certain subjects or who have missed classes are offered remedial classes in the afternoons from 2 to 4.
